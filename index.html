Spreadsheet Integration:

To use this calculator from your spreadsheet:

  1. Create a hyperlink with this format: ${window.location.origin}${window.location.pathname}?loadDate=YYYY-MM-DD&weight=1000&distance=500
  2. Replace the values with cell references in your spreadsheet
  3. Click the copy button to copy the dates
